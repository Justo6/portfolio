About me

I think of myself primarily as a creative thinker. Thus, I enjoy seeing an idea go from its inception to a finished product because it is those processes that allow me to express myself creatively.

I find myself invested in programming because of that very same reason. It is liberating and empowering as a tool, and, most importantly, fun. I first got introduced to programming through a JavaScript course on Code Academy. A few other online courses later, in addition to the intense preparation that I received at LearningFuze’s Coding Bootcamp, I am more confident than ever in that I’ve made the right choice in pursuing web development as a career.

Currently, I’m looking for a web development position where I can feel challenged every day and grow as a developer. I enjoy working on problems that impact everyday people and I’m seeking a company that matches that vision.
